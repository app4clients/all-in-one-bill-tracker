## Blogger Setup (Simple)

1. Open Blogger dashboard.
2. Go to `Pages` and create a new page named `All-in-One Bill Tracker`.
3. Switch editor to `HTML view`.
4. Copy the full content of `BLOGGER_SALES_PAGE.html` and paste it.
5. Publish the page.
6. Add the page to your menu from `Layout` or `Pages` settings.
7. Replace the Google Play URL with your real app listing URL.

## Optional

1. Add a custom domain in Blogger settings for better branding.
2. Add Google Analytics or Meta Pixel from Blogger `Theme -> Edit HTML` if needed.
